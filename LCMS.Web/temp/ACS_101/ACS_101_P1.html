Learning Objectives

  • Distinguish between an insurance company's individual and organizational customers and between internal and external customers.
  • Describe factors, including non-industry service expectations, that influence customer expectations and perceptions.
  • Distinguish between basic customer service and exceptional customer service, and explain how exceptional customer service can help bridge the service gap.
  • Explain why providing exceptional customer experiences is a top priority for insurers, and describe the challenges involved in delivering exceptional experiences.
